Venezia Marble

Venezia Marble specializes in providing you with the best there is in fabrication and installation for your kitchen counter-tops as well as bathroom vanities, fireplaces, and bars.

All Fabrication is done in Ridgefield with additional slabs sent over railroad track located behind the showroom. There are many varieties and colors of granite and marble.

Visit our showroom to shop for hundreds of different colors and designs. We also carry many lines of Natural Stone Tiles, Ceramic, Porcelain and Glass tiles to better serve you with your kitchen, bathroom or fireplace. From start to finish, we do our own installation to best serve you.

Since 2001 Venezia Marble has been proudly serving the Connecticut Fairfield County areas like Ridgefield, Weston, Wilton, New Canaan, Redding, Darien, Easton, Fairfield, Stamford, Norwalk, New Fairfield, Bethel, Danbury, as well as New York State, South Salem, North Salem, Brewster.

Please come in to Showroom in Ridgefied or call to get your project started. We will be happy to work with you.
